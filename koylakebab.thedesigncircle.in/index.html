About Us

Koyla Kebab has been serving you our Popular, Well Loved, & Traditional Melt in your Mouth Kebabs for Over 10 years now…

As we transition into our new Avatar, we continue to serve you YOUR Old Favourites, such as our Famous Slow-Cooked Kebabs & Kakoris, along with new additions such as our Biryani In a Bowl section, with our very own Koyla twist, our Mutton Burrah Kebab, the Tandoori Jhinga, & YOUR soon to be Veggie Favourite, Malai Broccoli Kebab! We are also introducing our Koyla Lab Section, where we hope to showcase some of our Creativity, by adding Interesting & Innovative dishes, to keep YOU, our guests coming back for more! Novel takes on Koyla’s Traditional Flavours! Trust us, you don’t want to miss this!
